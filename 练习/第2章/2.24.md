解释如何将等式(2.9) 和等式(2.10) 应用到这些示例上。
$$
B2U_k[x_{k-1},x_{k-2},..., x_0] = B2U_w([x_{w-1},x_{w-2},...,x_0]) \ mod \ 2^k \tag {2.9}
$$
$$
B2T_k[x_{k-1},x_{k-2},..., x_0] = U2T_k(B2U_w([x_{w-1},x_{w-2},...,x_0]) \ mod \ 2^k) \tag {2.10}
$$

$w = 4,k = 3,2^k=2^3 = 8$
